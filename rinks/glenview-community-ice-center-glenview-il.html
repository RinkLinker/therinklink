About this rink

Year-round. Three sheets: 2 NHL (200x85) + 1 studio. Main rink: 550 seats, 6 locker rooms. Second rink: 150 seats, 5 locker rooms. On-site: Offensive Zone Hockey Pro Shop, North Branch restaurant (seats 50), Joe Donut grab-and-go, dryland training area, dance/birthday studio. Home to Glenview Stars youth hockey and area high school teams. One of the premier multi-sheet facilities in Illinois.
