About this rink

Year-round events/convention center; ice installed September through March for hockey season. NHL-size rink (200x85 ft), new in 2019. Home to Shawano Hockey League Jr. Hawks and Shawano/Bonduel High School Hawks co-op team. LiveBarn available for high school games. Volunteer-run non-profit.
