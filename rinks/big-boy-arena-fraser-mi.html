Big Boy Arena

About this rink

Formerly Fraser Hockeyland / Great Lakes Sports City. 5 ice sheets including a 3,400-seat stadium rink. Second-largest ice rink complex in America. Home to Big Boy Hockey Club, Metro Jets (USPHL), Fraser Figure Skating Club, and Michigan Sports Enterprises adult leagues. B&R Sports pro shop and Michigan Athletic Training (MAT) on site. Hosted 2007 USA Hockey National Championships.
