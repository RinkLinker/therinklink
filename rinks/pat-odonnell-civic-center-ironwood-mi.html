About this rink

Rebuilt 2020 on campus of Gogebic Community College and Mt. Zion Ski Hill. Upgraded locker rooms, infrared-heated bleachers, licensed kitchen for concessions. Home to Ironwood Lumberjacks (Tier II Junior A hockey) and community programs. Also hosts trade shows, concerts, seasonal sports practices, and community events.
