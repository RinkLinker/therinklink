About this rink

Community Rink (300 seats) at Pegula Ice Arena complex. Public skating, freestyle, stick time, adult open skate, learn-to-play, power skating. LiveBarn venue. Skate sharpening available. The Varsity Rink (6,014 seats, home of Penn State Nittany Lions NCAA D1) is excluded — main NCAA bowl. $88M facility funded by Kim and Terry Pegula.
