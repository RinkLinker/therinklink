About this rink

93,000 sq ft municipal facility. 3 sheets: 2 full NHL-size (85x200) + 1 studio sheet (57x145). Renovated 2010. Home to UW-Eau Claire Blugolds men's and women's hockey (NCAA DIII) and Eau Claire Youth Hockey. LiveBarn available (youth games; UWEC streams privately). Fitness center, mezzanine viewing, meeting/party rooms.

Spectator info

Main rink seats 1,400; second rink seats 500.
