Novi Ice Arena

About this rink

Opened September 1998. Two NHL-regulation sheets. 8 locker rooms. Pro shop, two cafés, skate rental, meeting/party rooms. Home to Novi Youth Hockey Association, Skating Club of Novi, Novi HS, and Northville HS hockey.

Spectator info

Main rink seats 750; second rink seats 250. Heated viewing areas.
