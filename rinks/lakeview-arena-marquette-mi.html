Marquette Hockey

About this rink

Opened 1974. City-owned multi-purpose arena. Former home of NMU hockey (1976-1999) and Marquette Rangers (NAHL, 2006-2010). Won Kraft Hockeyville USA 2016 — received $150K in upgrades and hosted Buffalo Sabres vs. Carolina Hurricanes NHL preseason game (Oct. 4, 2016). Now home to Marquette Junior Hockey, Marquette Mutineers (GLHL), and community programs. Also hosts concerts, wrestling, and large events. Pro shop (Superior Hockey) on site.
