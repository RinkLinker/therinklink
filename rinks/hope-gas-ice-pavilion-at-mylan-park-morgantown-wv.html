About this rink

Opened Sept 8, 2025. 48,000 sq ft. NHL-sized surface (some sources say Olympic-sized). 906–1,200 permanent seats. 8 locker rooms, 2 meeting rooms, concessions, skate rental area. $15-16M project funded by Hope Gas, Hazel Ruby McQuain Charitable Trust, Mylan Park Foundation. Home of WVU Club Hockey (5 teams), Morgantown Hockey Association, area high school teams. Public skating Sat/Sun. Curling and learn-to-skate programming. Designed to accommodate a 2nd sheet if demand warrants. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Hope Gas Ice Arena' — correct name is Hope Gas Ice Pavilion.
