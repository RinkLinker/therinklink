About this rink

Three indoor rinks: 1980 Herb Brooks Arena (site of 'Miracle on Ice'), 1932 Jack Shea Arena, and USA Rink. Year-round public skating, figure skating, stick & puck. Speed skating oval (outdoor) also on-site. $100M renovation completed 2023 for Winter World University Games. Olympic Museum on-site.
