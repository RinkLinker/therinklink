About this rink

Only indoor ice rink in Southeast Alaska. City and Borough of Juneau operated. Located on Douglas Island, connected to Juneau by bridge. Public skating, stick & puck, pond hockey, figure skating, speed skating clinics, youth and adult hockey. Public skate: $7 adult / $6 under 18 / $5 senior. Skate rental included in admission.
