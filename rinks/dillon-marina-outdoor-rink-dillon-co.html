About this rink

FREE community ice rink on the Dillon Reservoir at Dillon Marina. Open sunrise to sundown. 136 ft x 70 ft natural ice with stunning views of Lake Dillon and Tenmile/Gore Ranges. Night lighting until 9pm. NO skate rentals on-site - bring your own. Drop-in hockey and broomball organized seasonally. NOTE: The Lake Loops trails on the reservoir did not open in 2025-26 season due to late freeze. Check townofdillon.com for current season status.
