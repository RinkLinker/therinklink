About this rink

Built 1976; renovated 1995. On LSSU campus (Norris Center complex). Named for hometown hockey legend Clarence "Taffy" Abel. Capacity 4,000 — only on-campus arena in US with seats exceeding school enrollment. Home of Lake Superior State Lakers (CCHA; 5 national championships). Raucous home-ice atmosphere.
