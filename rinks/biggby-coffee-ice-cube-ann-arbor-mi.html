About this rink

Opened 1995. 155,000 sq ft. Two NHL + one Olympic-sized rink. Over 850,000 visitors annually. Home to Biggby Coffee Hockey Club, Ann Arbor Figure Skating Club, Huron/Pioneer/Saline/Father Gabriel Richard HS hockey, EMU Club Hockey, and Wolverine Sports Club Speed Skating. On-site: Perani's Hockey World pro shop, Chop Detroit café, physical therapy (MedSport), fitness center, arcade. Operated by Black Bear Sports Group.
