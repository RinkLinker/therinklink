About this rink

Moffat County facility, managed by Colorado Extreme Hockey Association since September 2024. 85x125 ft ice surface (not full NHL size). Seasonal Oct–mid-March. CASH OR CHECK ONLY — no credit cards accepted. 4 dressing rooms, bleachers. Public skating, stick & puck (drop-in hockey), Craig Youth Hockey League, and curling on Fridays.
