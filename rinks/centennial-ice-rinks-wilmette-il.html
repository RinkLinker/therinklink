About this rink

Opened 1977. Operated by Wilmette Park District inside the Centennial Recreation Complex (shared with Wilmette Tennis Club and Family Aquatic Center). One full NHL sheet + one studio sheet. ISI National award-winning facility for management and ice-skating programs. Named #1 ice rink by North Shore Magazine readers. Instructs 3,000+ skaters/year. Home to Wilmette Jr. Trevians Hockey (WHA), Wilmette Tribe Hockey Club, and New Trier High School Hockey Club.
