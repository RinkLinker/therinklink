About this rink

Operated by Kankakee Valley Park District. NHL sheet. Breakaway Café concessions, Game Zone arcade, 4 private party/meeting rooms, state-of-the-art fitness center. Programs: public skate, learn to skate, figure skating, intro to hockey, youth hockey (Kankakee Coyotes), adult hockey, curling (Ice Valley Curling), drop-in, freestyle. Little Blackhawks Learn to Play partner. 50 miles south of Chicago.
