About this rink

Historic 4,001-seat arena opened 1950. Original filming location of 'Slap Shot.' Home of Johnstown Tomahawks (NAHL) and Pitt-Johnstown Ice Cats (CHE). Four local high school teams use arena as home ice. Won 2015 Kraft Hockeyville USA. Hosts youth and adult tournaments, veterans hockey. ⚠️ CSV listed as two entries (1st Summit + Cambria County War Memorial Practice Rink) — one building, one record.
