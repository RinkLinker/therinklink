About this rink

Only ice rink in Boulder. Part of CU Boulder Student Recreation Center. Must have Rec Center membership to skate. Membership required for all users - students, staff, and community members. Drop-in hockey requires helmet with full face shield, gloves, shin guards, skates, long pants. | On-site shop: CU Skate Counter
