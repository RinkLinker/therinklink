About this rink

Two-sheet arena operated by Ramsey County. North rink maintains ice year-round; South rink seasonal (mid-Oct through mid-Mar, converts to dry floor in warm months). Home to Highland hockey programs. Formerly known as Highland Ice Arena. Named after Peanuts creator Charles M. Schulz, a St. Paul native.
