About this rink

KC Parks & Recreation. 34,000 sq ft community center with indoor ice. Located in Frank Vaydik Park. Year-round. LiveBarn venue. Home of Line Creek Figure Skating Club and KC Fighting Saints Youth Hockey. $8 admission, $3 skate rental.
