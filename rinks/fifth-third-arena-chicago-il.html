About this rink

Opened 2017. Official practice home of the Chicago Blackhawks (NHL). $65M expansion opened early 2026, adding 2 new sheets for a total of 4. Expansion includes Championship Rink (2,000 seats) — home of Chicago Steel (USHL) starting 2026-27 season — plus Rocky's Bar (tribute to Rocky Wirtz), Blackhawks Hall of Fame, lounge spaces, skyline patio, and all-day café. 440,000+ annual visitors. Each rink seats ~275 with ADA-friendly boards and synthetic ice for sled hockey. Pro shop, off-ice training, public skating, adult leagues, youth programming.
