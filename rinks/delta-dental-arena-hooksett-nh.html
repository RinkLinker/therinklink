About this rink

78,000 sq ft. Two NHL-size sheets. Opened 1998. North arena renamed Ryan Frew Memorial Rink (2021). Home of NH Mountain Kings. Formerly Tri-Town Ice Arena — renamed Delta Dental Arena Sept 2025 per naming rights with Northeast Delta Dental. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Tri-Town Ice Arena' — corrected to current name.
