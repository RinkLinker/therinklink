About this rink

Opened August 2005. 70,000 sq ft. Two NHL sheets (200x85). Home to Macomb Hockey Club, Eisenhower Eagles, Romeo Bulldogs, Utica Chieftains HS hockey, Onyx-Suburban Skating Academy, and Macomb Adult Hockey League. On-site: Centre Ice Café, Perani's Hockey World pro shop, 10 locker rooms.
