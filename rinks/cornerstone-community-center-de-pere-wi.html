About this rink

3 sheets: Hilliard/Weyers Arena (NHL, 1,674 seats), Schneider Arena (NHL), Resch Olympic Pavilion (Olympic). Home to St. Norbert College Green Knights (NCAA DIII), Green Bay Gamblers (USHL) practices, GBAYHA, and DC Everest youth programs. 4th sheet (Donald & Patricia Schneider Family Arena, 3,000 seats) under construction — expected fall 2026. Has hosted 8 USA Hockey National Championships.

Spectator info

Main rink (Hilliard/Weyers Arena) seats 1,674; standing room to ~2,000.
