About this rink

Two NHL-size rinks. Originally built 1971 with one indoor + one outdoor rink; second sheet enclosed in 1995 as part of $3.5M renovation. Named for Royal Oak's 16-year Director of Parks & Recreation. Home to Royal Oak Hockey Club, New Edge Figure Skating Club, M1 Griffins HS Hockey, Brother Rice HS, and Bishop Foley HS hockey.
