About this rink

Two surfaces: indoor NHL-size rink (year-round, 800 spectator seating) + outdoor covered NHL-size Ice Pavilion (seasonal Nov–early Mar, added 2012, $1.5M). Formerly Ice Sports KC. $1M indoor renovation 2011. Home of KC Stars and KC Jets Youth Hockey Association. Summer: Pavilion used for inline hockey (KC Cobras). ⚠️ CSV listed as 1 sheet Indoor — corrected to 2 surfaces (1 indoor + 1 covered outdoor seasonal).
