About this rink

Municipal multipurpose facility opened 2010. 2 ice sheets (one year-round, one seasonal). Capacity ~1,300. Home to Onalaska youth hockey, high school hockey, and adult leagues. Also offers basketball, volleyball, batting cages, meeting/convention space, and catering. Former home of Coulee Region Chill (NAHL, 2010-2014). Skate sharpening and pro shop on site.
