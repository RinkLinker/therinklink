About this rink

Non-profit, all-volunteer facility owned and operated by the Rhinelander Ice Association. Home of the Rhinelander Hodags high school hockey. Ample parking for ~1,500 spectators. Skate rentals available.

Spectator info

Capacity ~700 with ice; ~3,000 for dry floor events.
