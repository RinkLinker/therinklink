About this rink

San Francisco's only year-round indoor ice rink. NHL-size sheet. Public skating, skating school, adult hockey leagues, synchronized skating (the Tremors), figure skating. Bowling alley also on-site. ⚠️ Management changing in summer 2026 — rink closing July 2026 for renovations, reopening ~August 2026 under Yerba Buena Gardens Conservancy. Inspired the hockey arena in Pixar's Inside Out 2 (2024). Admission $14; skate rental $5.
