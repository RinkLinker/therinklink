About this rink

30,000 sq ft outdoor natural ice rink - the largest and highest man-made rink in Colorado, at 10,152 ft elevation (highest incorporated city in North America). Operated by Lake County Parks, Recreation & Open Space Dept. Features skate and hockey equipment rentals, snack bar, 2,300 sq ft warming hut, fire pit, and great sound system. Programs include youth/adult hockey leagues, curling, women's and co-ed drop-in hockey, figure skating lessons. Address is also listed as 505 W 5th St. Season: Dec-Feb (weather permitting). Check lakecountyco.gov for current schedule.
