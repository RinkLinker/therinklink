About this rink

Full NHL-regulation-size indoor rink. Open to YMCA members and non-members. Public skating, stick & puck, open hockey (no-contact), learn-to-skate, youth sports leagues. Albany Figure Skating Club trains here. Non-member open skate: $5 adults / $4 students / $3 under 5. Rentals $2. ⚠️ Not in source CSV — added from Albany Kid Capital Region ice skating guide.
