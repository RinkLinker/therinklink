About this rink

501(c)(3) nonprofit. Built 2014 on Sanford Sports Complex. Three NHL sheets. Official practice facility of Sioux Falls Stampede (USHL), Sioux Falls Power, and Sioux Falls Flyers. Home of Sioux Falls Youth Hockey Association, Figure Skating Club, Legend Hockey, Jr. Stampede AAA. Open skate, sled hockey, figure skating, curling, birthday parties. Stick-n-puck Mon/Wed/Fri first-come-first-served.
