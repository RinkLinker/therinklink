About this rink

Picturesque outdoor pond rink in the heart of Winter Park Resort's alpine village. FREE admission with your own skates. Skate rentals available at Adventure & Supply Co. (~$19/hr). Glow Night Cosmic Skating every Thu/Fri/Sat 5-7pm with DJ and colorful lights. Open mid-November to mid-April. Views of the Continental Divide. Ice maintained daily by resort. | On-site shop: Adventure & Supply Co.
