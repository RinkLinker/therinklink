About this rink

City of Durango Parks & Recreation. Indoor refrigerated rink open Oct-May for ice, summer for roller skating. Has attached ski hill with 2 rope tows, 500 vertical feet of terrain (Dec-Mar). Disco Night Fri/Sat evenings. Home to Durango Area Youth Hockey Association (DAYHA), Fort Lewis College Club Hockey, adult leagues, Durango figure skating. Closed major holidays. Birthday parties available.
