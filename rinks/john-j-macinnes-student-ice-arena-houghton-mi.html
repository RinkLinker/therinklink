About this rink

Opened 1971-72 season. Named for coach John J. MacInnes (555-295-39 record). Capacity 4,466 (top 30 NCAA hockey). 14 luxury suites, 6 concession areas, pro shop, 9 locker rooms (5 with direct ice access), Puck Pit training area. Home of Michigan Tech Huskies (Big Ten; 3 national championships: 1975, 1976, 2023). Public skating, open hockey, and freestyle available year-round.
