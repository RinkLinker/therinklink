About this rink

Two-sheet facility. Formerly RDV Ice Den / RDV Sportsplex Ice Den. Now operating as Orlando Ice Den. Part of the larger RDV Sportsplex complex (athletic club, Olympic pool, rehab center). Full hockey programming and public skating. Home of Orlando Jr. Solar Bears.
