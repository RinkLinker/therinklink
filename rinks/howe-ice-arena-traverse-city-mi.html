About this rink

Single-sheet arena inside the Grand Traverse County Civic Center. Home to Traverse City West Titans and Traverse City Central Trojans high school hockey, adult leagues, learn to skate, and public skating. Operated by I.C.E. (non-profit). Note: nearby Centre Ice Arena (2 sheets, Detroit Red Wings Training Camp home) is a companion facility not yet in this directory.
