About this rink

Two parallel NHL-sized arenas connected at ends by studio rinks — creating a unique 1/5-mile 'CooLLoop.' 1,000-seat primary arena + 450-seat secondary arena. Home of New Mexico Ice Wolves (NAHL). Public skating, hockey, figure skating, speed skating, broomball, sled hockey. Pro shop, snack shop, weight training & ballet room. At the base of the Sandia Mountains.
