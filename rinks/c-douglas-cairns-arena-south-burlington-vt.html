About this rink

Twin rink facility. Rink 1: 600 seats; Rink 2: 400 seats. Both NHL-size (85'×200'), 4 locker rooms per rink. Pro shop (hockey basics, Jackson/Riedell figure skates), café (cash only). Home of St. Michael's College and UVM club hockey. Low-cost public skating.
