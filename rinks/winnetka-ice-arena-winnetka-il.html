About this rink

Built 1972. Operated by Winnetka Park District. Seasonal (September–May). One full NHL sheet with bleacher seating, skate rental, lounge. LiveBarn equipped. Home to Winnetka Warriors (WHC), North Shore HC, and North Shore Warhawks youth hockey. Strong figure skating programs.
