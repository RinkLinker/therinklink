About this rink

Two NHL-size rinks. Owned by the Grand Rapids Griffins organization (Detroit Red Wings AHL affiliate). Home to Hope College hockey, Holland Hockey Association, and Holland Ice Dogs. On-site: SK8's Pro Shop. Public skating, drop-in hockey, figure skating, adult leagues. Renamed from Griff's IceHouse West to Holland Ice Arena in Feb 2026 under new Black Bear Sports Group ownership. Millions in facility improvements planned.
