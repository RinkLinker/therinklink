About this rink

Natural non-refrigerated ice arena in the heart of Crested Butte. Managed by Crested Butte Nordic Center (next door). Skate rentals available at Nordic Center warming house. Active recreational and youth hockey Dec–March. Home to West Elk Hockey Association teams.
