About this rink

Built 1923; converted to ice arena 1973. One of the most iconic college hockey venues in the country. Home of Michigan Wolverines (Big Ten; 9 national championships). Rink named Red Berenson Rink (2017). Last renovated 2012 ($16M). Public skating, open hockey, and skate rental available. Home to AAAHA recreational leagues, UM club hockey, synchronized skating, and several Ann Arbor area HS teams.

Spectator info

Seating capacity 5,800 (total capacity ~6,600).
