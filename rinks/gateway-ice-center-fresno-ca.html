About this rink

Fresno's only year-round ice rink. Serving Fresno since 1995. Home of Fresno Monsters, Fresno Youth Hockey Club, Fresno Junior Monsters, and Ice Skating Club of Fresno (USFSA member, founded 1949). Public skating, hockey leagues, figure skating, broomball, birthday parties, school field trips.
