About this rink

Formerly Rostraver Ice Garden — second oldest ice rink in Pennsylvania (opened 1965). Black Bear Sports Group purchased Feb 2023, renamed cfsBank Event Center. 65,000 sq ft, 5,000-seat capacity. One NHL sheet (converts to dry floor spring/summer). Bubba's Kitchen + Bar. Home of Mon Valley Thunder youth hockey, PennWest California hockey. Won 2017 Kraft Hockeyville. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Rostraver Ice Garden' — corrected to current name. Address corrected from 101 to 111 Gallitin Rd.
