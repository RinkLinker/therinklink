About this rink

Gunnison Valley's premier winter venue. Includes indoor ice facility (open late Sep–Mar), outdoor ice rink, and free Pac Man Pond for public skating daily. Also features Nordic trails and jib park. Home to West Elk Hockey Association. Indoor rink manager: Andy Eflin (970) 641-0435. Full gear recommended for open hockey; helmet & gloves required for stick & puck. | On-site shop: Concessions area
