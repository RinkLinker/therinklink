About this rink

Replaced 52-year-old South Suburban Ice Arena. Three sheets: center sheet seats 800, second sheet 220, third sheet also available. Restaurant/bar with ice views. Drop-in hockey (adult & youth) $10.50 res/$11.50 non-res.
