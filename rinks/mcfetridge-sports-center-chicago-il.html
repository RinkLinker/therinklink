About this rink

Chicago Park District facility. Two sheets: main rink and studio rink. Adult recreational hockey leagues on main rink; Stick & Puck on studio rink (Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun). Figure skating classes, synchronized skating, open skate. Skate rental available ($3). Skate sharpening in pro shop. Open year-round except August.
