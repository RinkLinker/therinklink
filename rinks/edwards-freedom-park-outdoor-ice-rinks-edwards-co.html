About this rink

Community outdoor ice rinks at Edwards Freedom Park, managed by Mountain Recreation District with dedicated volunteer Tom Boyd. Now in their 4th+ season. Two rinks: a larger hockey rink and a smaller general skating rink, both built on turf using a specialized board/liner system. FREE to skate. Loaner skates available in storage boxes near baseball dugouts (first-come, limited supply). Golf-cart Zamboni maintained by volunteers. Part of a beloved neighborhood tradition — kids skate after school, adults at lunch. Contact Mountain Rec at (970) 766-5555.
