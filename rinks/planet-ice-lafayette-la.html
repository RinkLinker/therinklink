About this rink

Only permanent indoor ice rink in Louisiana. Opened 2011. Home of Louisiana Drillers (NA3HL), LAHA Youth Hockey (ages 4–16), Figure Skating Club of Louisiana (USFSA member since 2019), Baton Rouge United Hockey Association (BRUHA), and the Bayou Battle League. Fraser Cup champion facility (2025). Public skating, learn-to-skate, birthday parties. ⚠️ CSV listed website as planeticearena.com — corrected to planeticerinkla.com.
