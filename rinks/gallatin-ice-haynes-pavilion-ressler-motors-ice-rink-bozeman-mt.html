About this rink

Two sheets at Gallatin County Fairgrounds managed by BAHA (Bozeman Amateur Hockey Association): Haynes Pavilion (Bottcher Memorial Ice Rink) + Ressler Motors Ice Rink (The Ice Barn). Seasonal: Oct–Apr. Hockey, figure skating, curling, learn-to-skate. Home of Bozeman Icedogs Junior A, MSU Bobcat Hockey Club, Senior A Stingers. CSV listed as two separate entries — merged into one 2-sheet record.
