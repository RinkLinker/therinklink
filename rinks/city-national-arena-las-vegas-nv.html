About this rink

Official practice facility of Vegas Golden Knights (NHL). 146,000 sq ft. Two NHL regulation rinks (Summerlin Hospital Rink + Ghost Energy Rink), each seating 600. Team headquarters, locker rooms, MacKenzie River Pizza Grill & Pub, pro shop. Public skating $10/$7 military. Walkers free. Hosts Jr. Golden Knights, UNLV, VGK Center of Excellence figure skating, Western States Hockey League.
