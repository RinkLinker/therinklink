About this rink

Opened Feb 2010. Two sheets: main NHL-size rink + Annex Studio Rink (added 2014, with mezzanine viewing and dryland training). Former official Blackhawks practice facility (2010–2018). Heated indoor parking garage. 4 large locker rooms with individual stalls and showers, plus dedicated women's locker room. Stanley Club Bar & Grill overlooks ice. Pro shop. One of the most iconic adult hockey facilities in the Midwest.
