About this rink

NHL-sized partially enclosed/covered outdoor refrigerated rink at the Fraser Valley Sports Complex. Managed by Fraser Valley Metropolitan Recreation District. Features skate rentals, warming house, indoor concessions, restrooms. Strong youth hockey leagues. Fraser is known as the 'Icebox of the Nation' for its extreme cold. Also has ball fields, volleyball, soccer. Call ahead for open skating times as they are limited.
