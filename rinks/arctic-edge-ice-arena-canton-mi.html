About this rink

Two NHL-size rinks. Home to Canton Hockey Association, Victory Honda AAA program, Canton HS hockey, Willow Run/Belleville HS, Plymouth-Canton-Salem Girls Hockey, Arctic Figure Skating Club, and American Hockey Academy. Training facility for Olympic/World figure skaters including Meryl Davis, Charlie White, Maia & Alex Shibutani, Nathan Chen, Gracie Gold. Strength & conditioning facility on site.
