About this rink

Outdoor partially covered ice rink at the Alamosa Family Recreation Center. Seasonal (Thanksgiving–Feb, weather permitting). Hockey leagues hosted here. Skate rentals available. All skating first come first served - not reservable. Ice rink passes available Oct 1. For info contact Alamosa Family Recreation Center.
