About this rink

Founded 1979. Only dual ice and roller skating facility in Arkansas; only ice rink in Central Arkansas. NHL-regulation size ice surface (17,000 sq ft). Year-round: public skating, figure skating, youth and adult hockey leagues, broomball, roller derby. Pro shop, snack bar, spectator seating. Official home of Little Rock Aces Hockey Club.
