About this rink

Opened 1997. Two NHL-size rinks. Official practice facility of South Carolina Stingrays (ECHL). Full service pro shop, Penalty Box Sports Lounge, concessions, meeting rooms. LiveBarn venue. Home of Charleston Youth Hockey Association and Figure Skating Club of Charleston. USA Hockey Pee Wee National Championship host. Public skating $12 weekends, $10 weekdays; $5/$4 skate rental.
