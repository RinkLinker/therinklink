About this rink

Three sheets: Edge II (735 E Jefferson), Edge on John (545 John St), and Water's Edge. Former official practice home of Chicago Blackhawks (NHL). Former home of Chicago Steel (USHL, 2000-2015). 3,000-seat main arena. Home to Chicago Blues adult hockey leagues, Roosevelt Lakers college hockey (ACHA D1 men's & women's), and multiple HS programs. Also houses The Water's Edge Aquatic Center.
