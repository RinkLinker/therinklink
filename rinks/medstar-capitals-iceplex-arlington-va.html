About this rink

Official practice facility of Washington Capitals (NHL). Highest ice rink above street-level in the US — 8th floor atop Ballston Quarter parking garage. 137,000 sq ft. Two NHL sheets, 8 locker rooms, 20,000 sq ft Capitals training center, ProShop, Capitals Team Store. Metro-accessible (Ballston). Home of Georgetown and GWU club teams, NOVA Cool Cats special hockey, DC Sled Sharks. 300 Capitals practice hours/year; 12,000 hours total ice annually. Not fully handicapped accessible. Note: formerly Kettler Capitals Iceplex.
