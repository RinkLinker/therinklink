About this rink

Natural non-refrigerated ice pond at Rotary Park in Montrose. City of Montrose Parks & Cemeteries maintains the rink when conditions allow — typically 1–6 weeks per winter depending on weather. 100x200 ft cleared area. FREE admission, NO skate rentals on-site. Ice safety verified by city staff (3-inch drill test). Locals drop in for hockey and broomball. Skate at your own risk per posted signage. Contact City of Montrose Parks at (970) 249-7505.
