About this rink

Completed 2020. Award-winning 130,000 sq ft community center ($55M project). Two full NHL sheets. Also includes library branch, two full gyms, 200-meter running track, dance studio, 9 locker rooms, art room, childcare, outdoor sports fields. Home to Northwestern Wildcats men's hockey and synchronized skating teams (practice). Evanston Township HS hockey. Public skating $3–$7. Named best project by American Public Works Association (2021) and Chicago AIA top design.
