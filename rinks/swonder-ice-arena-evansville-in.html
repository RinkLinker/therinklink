About this rink

Opened Oct 2002. Two NHL-size sheets, 1,500 seating. 10 locker rooms. 10,000 sq ft fitness center with running track upstairs. Home to Evansville Youth Hockey, Tri-State Adult Hockey, River City Ice Theatre, Greater Evansville Figure Skating Club, USI Ice Eagles. One sheet open year-round. City of Evansville operated.
