About this rink

Two-sheet year-round facility. Founded 2000. LiveBarn equipped. Full hockey programming: public skate, youth and adult leagues, pickup hockey, broomball. Home of University of Louisville Cards Ice Hockey, Louisville Skating Academy (figure skating), and Louisville Stallions youth hockey. Pro shop on-site.
