About this rink

Northwest Arkansas's only indoor ice rink. 220,000 sq ft community recreation center. Home of University of Arkansas ACHA D2/D3 hockey club (Razorbacks). Public skating, hockey leagues, learn-to-skate. Two pools, gym, fitness center, food court also on-site. Trailhead on Razorback Greenway.
