About this rink

Indiana's only three-sheet facility. Opened 2010. Formerly SportONE Parkview Icehouse. Home of Fort Wayne Komets (ECHL) practice, Indiana Tech Warriors (NCAA), Fort Wayne Spacemen (USPHL), Fort Wayne Youth Hockey, and High School Hockey Association. Overtime Bar + Grill overlooks all three rinks. 12 locker rooms. On-site pro shop.
