About this rink

Established 1961. Indy Parks' only indoor ice rink. NHL-size sheet, 4 locker rooms, concession stand. Seasonal: September through April. Home to South Stars HS Hockey Club, South Indy Youth Hockey Association, Total Package Hockey Center of Excellence, Sycamore Ice Skating Club. Admission: children $5, adults $7, skate rental $3.
