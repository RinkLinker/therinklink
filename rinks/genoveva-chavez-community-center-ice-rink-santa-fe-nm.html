About this rink

City of Santa Fe municipal rink. NHL-size sheet with rare reverse osmosis water system (only ~2% of US rinks) producing glass-like ice favored for competitions. $3 skate rental. Home of Santa Fe Skating Club. Reopened November 2025 after extended repairs to cooling towers and dehumidifier. Has had recurring mechanical issues — flag for status check before Supabase launch.
