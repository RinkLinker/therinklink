About this rink

Renamed from Wells Fargo Arena to Casey's Center in June 2025. 15,181-seat AHL arena. Home of Iowa Wild (AHL affiliate of Minnesota Wild). Also home to Iowa Wolves (NBA G League) and Iowa Barnstormers. Opened 2005. Part of Iowa Events Center complex. No public skating — hockey events only.
