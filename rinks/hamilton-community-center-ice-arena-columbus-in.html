About this rink

Built 1958, designed by architect Harry Weese. Enclosed 1975-76. Renovated 2015 ($2.8M). NHL sheet + studio practice rink (2 sheets total). Year-round. Home of Columbus Icemen HS hockey, Columbus Flames youth hockey, Lincoln Center Skate Club, Full Throttle Speedskating, Columbus Broomball Association. Home of Indiana Sentinels (FPHL) starting 2025-26. Columbus Parks & Recreation operated.
