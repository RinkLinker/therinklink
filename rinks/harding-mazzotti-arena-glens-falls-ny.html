About this rink

4,794-seat ECHL arena. Home of Adirondack Thunder (ECHL, Calgary Flames affiliate). Built 1979. Formerly Cool Insuring Arena and Glens Falls Civic Center. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Cool Insuring Arena' — renamed Harding Mazzotti Arena after naming rights expired 2024. Heritage Hall banquet facility on-site.
