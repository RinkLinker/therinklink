About this rink

Historic rink in downtown Aspen for 50+ years. 82x184 ft floor. 150 bleacher seats. Only open during programmed hours. Dry floor events available June-August. For year-round skating use Lewis Ice Arena at ARC. Contact Ryan Loeb at ryan.loeb@aspen.gov for events/rentals. | On-site shop: Aspen Ice Garden
