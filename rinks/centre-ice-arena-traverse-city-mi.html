About this rink

Opened 1999. Two sheets (David's Rink and West Rink). Operated by I.C.E. non-profit — same org as Howe Ice Arena. Home of Detroit Red Wings Annual Training Camp (every September) and NHL Prospect Tournament. Also home to Grand Traverse Hockey Association, Traverse City North Stars (USP3HL), Traverse City Figure Skating Club, and Traverse City Curling Club. Hosts TC West Titans and TC Central Trojans HS hockey, adult leagues, Learn to Skate, Open Skates, and Sticks & Pucks. Pro shop and concessions on site. Open year-round.
