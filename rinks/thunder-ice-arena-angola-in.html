About this rink

Opened 2017. 46,200 sq ft, 700-seat arena. Home of Trine Thunder NCAA DIII men's and women's hockey (NCHA). Only women's intercollegiate hockey program in Indiana. Steuben County YMCA and local schools also use facility. Pro shop, VIP lounge, weight/training rooms. Public skating and youth leagues.
