About this rink

501(c)(3) nonprofit. 46,500 sq ft. Two rinks: Snider Rink (NHL 200'x85') + Norqual Studio Rink (100'x60'). 6 locker rooms. Rinkside Café (beer, wine, pizza). Student study room. Adaptive sports locker room — first adaptive ice facility west of the Mississippi. Opened 2015. Near UCSB. Home of Jr. Hawks and Monarchs hockey leagues.
