About this rink

Wisconsin's oldest indoor hockey arena, built 1933. Known as "The Dome" — iconic wooden lamella truss roof on the National Register of Historic Places. Two sheets: original dome (NHL, ~2,000 seats) + Olympic sheet added 1995-96. Home to Northland Pines Eagles, Eagle River Recreation Association youth hockey, Eagle River Figure Skating Club, and the Wisconsin Hockey Hall of Fame (est. 1975). Pro shop on site.
