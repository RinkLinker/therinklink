About this rink

Opened Dec 2003. Two NHL sheets (85'×200'). 8 locker rooms with showers. Located on Bethlehem Steel SteelStacks campus in historic South Side. Full service pro shop, The Steel Pub sports bar attached (window view of rink). Home of LV Phantoms Youth, Penguin Figure Skating Club, Lehigh University men's hockey, Polar Bears Special Hockey. Public skating $15/$10 adults/children. Only rink offering daily public skating year-round in Lehigh Valley.
