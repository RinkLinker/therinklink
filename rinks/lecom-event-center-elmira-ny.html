About this rink

3,784-seat arena with two ice surfaces. Home of Elmira Mammoth (FPHL). Opened 2000 as Coach USA Center. Renamed First Arena 2004, LECOM Event Center July 2024. Full-service restaurant & bar, arcade, 31 luxury suites. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'First Arena' — corrected to LECOM Event Center.
