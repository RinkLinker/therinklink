About this rink

Seasonal outdoor skating rink on Hy-Vee Plaza, owned and operated by the Green Bay Packers. Adjacent to Lambeau Field. Paid admission required even with own skates. Skate rentals and ice bikes available. 46 Below Café on site (food & drinks). Open approximately November through March, weather permitting. No hockey — public/recreational skating only.

Spectator info

Skating trail weaves under Ariens Hill; fire pit area on rink.
