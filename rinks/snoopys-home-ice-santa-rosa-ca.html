About this rink

Iconic rink fully renovated. Official name: Redwood Empire Ice Arena. Home of Santa Rosa Flyers youth hockey. Named for Charles M. Schulz (Peanuts creator) who built the original rink in 1969. Smaller than NHL regulation dimensions.
