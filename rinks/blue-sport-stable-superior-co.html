About this rink

Formerly Sport Stable, rebranded as Blue Sport Stable. 186,000 sq ft, 1.6 million visitors/year. Two NHL-sized ice rinks + one small skill rink, turf field, 3 basketball courts (convert to 5 volleyball/12 pickleball), batting cages, 7 indoor golf simulators, coffee shop, Floyd's Barbershop, physical therapy, preschool. Home to RoughRiders Sports Club (hockey, baseball, basketball, softball, volleyball). Located in Downtown Superior off US-36. | On-site shop: Blue Sport Stable Pro Shop

Spectator info

The Eatery: Full restaurant & bar on-site. High-top bar seating overlooks the courts. Coffee shop also in the building.
