About this rink

Opened August 1999. 114,000 sq ft. Formerly the Onyx Ice Arena; joined Suburban Ice family 2016. Three NHL sheets. Home to Rochester Hockey Club, Onyx-Suburban Skating Academy, Rochester United HS, Stoney Creek HS, and Oakland University Golden Grizzlies (men's hockey). Full-service pro shop (Perani's Hockey World), concessions, skate rental, and arcade on site.
