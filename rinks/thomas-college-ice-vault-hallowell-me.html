About this rink

Rebranded as Thomas College Ice Vault in 2024 (formerly Camden National Bank Ice Vault). Central Maine's only year-round arena. Regulation size, pro shop, conference rooms, snack bar. Home of Maine Moose U18 midget team and Thomas College Men's and Women's Hockey. Largest sign in Maine on building exterior. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Camden National Bank Ice Vault' — name updated.
