About this rink

Formerly Portland Ice Arena. City of Portland municipal facility. NHL-regulation size. 750 seats (1,000 standing room). 4 locker rooms, skate rentals, sharpening, concessions. Season July–April. 200,000 annual users. Full hockey and figure skating programming.
