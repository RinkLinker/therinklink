About this rink

Built 1999 by US Olympic Gold Medalist Bob Suter. Two NHL sheets. 1,500-seat showcase rink. Major 2018 renovation added luxury suites, beer garden, HD video boards. Home to USHL Madison Capitols, Middleton High School, and Middleton youth hockey. Also known as Legacy20 Arena Middleton.
