About this rink

Hawaii's only ice rink. Founded 1982. NHL-size sheet near Pearl Harbor on Oahu. Reopened Dec 2022 after COVID closure with refreshed interior, new snack bar, new Zamboni. Public skating $19 (skate rental included). Youth and adult hockey leagues (USA Hockey registered), stick & puck, learn-to-skate, figure skating, birthday parties. Pro shop. Hosts Aloha Cup tournament (Dec) and Draft Tournament (March). 300+ USA Hockey registered members. Home of Hawaii high school hockey program.
