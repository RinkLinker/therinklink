About this rink

Opened 1996 (formerly Ice Sports Wichita). Two sheets: Olympic-size + NHL-size. 2,000 capacity. Full-length viewing windows. Pucks Sports Bar, pro sport store, party rooms, concessions. Youth and adult hockey leagues, figure skating academy, learn-to-skate, speed skating. Operated by Sports Facilities Companies. Public skate $7.
