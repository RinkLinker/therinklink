About this rink

City-owned enclosed facility at Mosinee Recreation Center Park. Seasonal ice (winter months). Home of Mosinee Pride/Hockey Club and Mosinee High School hockey. Also used for community gatherings and church picnics in summer. Hockey Association has adopted the park.
