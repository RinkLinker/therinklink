About this rink

Opened July 2006. 'A Pad' (Steve Cady Arena): 3,642 seats, home of Miami RedHawks NCAA D1 hockey and synchronized skating team. 'B Pad': multi-purpose recreational sheet. 13+ locker areas. Newlove Pro Shop. Named for Lloyd Goggin. Designed by same firm as Nationwide Arena. Jr. RedHawks youth hockey and Talawanda HS varsity also use facility.
