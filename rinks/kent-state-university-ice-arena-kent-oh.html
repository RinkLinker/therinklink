About this rink

Built 1970, renovated 2006-07 and 2023. One NHL sheet (200'x85'), 1,500 seating. ⚠️ Second rink eliminated in 2023 renovation (converted to music education facility). Home of Kent State ACHA D1 and D3 teams and Figure Skating Team. Three area high school teams and youth hockey also use facility. Skate rental included with admission. Meeting room, skate sharpening, snack bar.
