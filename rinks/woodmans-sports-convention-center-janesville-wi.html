About this rink

Opened September 2025. 140,000 sq ft facility built on former Sears site in Uptown Janesville Mall. Two ice sheets: 1,500-seat permanent main rink (Mercyhealth Arena) + multipurpose arena (250-seat, seasonal ice). Home to Janesville Jets (NAHL), Beloit College hockey, and Janesville Figure Skating Club. Replaces the old Janesville Ice Arena (1974–2025) which is now closed.
