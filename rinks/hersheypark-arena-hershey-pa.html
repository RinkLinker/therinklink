About this rink

Historic 1936 arena, 7,286-seat capacity. Original home of Hershey Bears AHL (1938–2002). Now home of Hershey Cubs (USPHL Premier), Jr. Bears, Lebanon Valley College (NCAA D3), Shippensburg University hockey. Hershey Bears still use as practice facility. Public skating weekends. Note: Giant Center (main AHL bowl next door) is excluded — no public access. ⚠️ CSV listed Hersheypark Arena + Giant Center Practice Rink as two entries at same address — consolidated into one record.
