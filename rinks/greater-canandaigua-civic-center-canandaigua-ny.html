About this rink

Non-profit. Opened 1992. 17,000 sq ft, 700–1,000 spectator seating. Home of Canandaigua Braves HS hockey, Canandaigua Knights youth hockey, Finger Lakes Freeze girls hockey. Public skating, adult leagues. Seasonal ice (fall/winter). Summer use for events. Also known as Ingle Ice Arena. ⚠️ Not in source CSV — added from Finger Lakes ice rink directory.
