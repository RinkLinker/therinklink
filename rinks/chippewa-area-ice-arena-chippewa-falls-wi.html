About this rink

Association-owned non-profit facility. 2 indoor sheets + 1 outdoor rink. Capacity ~1,280. Home to Chippewa Steel (NAHL), Chippewa Thunder youth hockey, and Chippewa Steel high school hockey. Concession stand, weight room. Outdoor rink hosts events including annual Beerfest.
