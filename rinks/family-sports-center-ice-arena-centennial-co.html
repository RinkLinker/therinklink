About this rink

150,000 sq ft entertainment complex. Colorado Avalanche practice facility - sometimes fans can watch. Two NHL sheets. Also has mini golf, laser tag, climbing wall, bumper cars, arcade. Part of South Suburban Parks and Recreation District (SSPRD). | On-site shop: Family Sports Pro Shop

Spectator info

Avalanche Grill: Full restaurant & bar on-site with views of the ice. Full menu, beer & wine. Open during all sessions.
