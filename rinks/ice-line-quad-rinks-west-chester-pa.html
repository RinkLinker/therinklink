Amenities

Skate Rental Locker Rooms (Multiple) Accessible Year-Round Restaurants: The Grille The Grille at ICE LINE is happy to serve our guests delicious, made-to-order food and beverages. Located in the lobby for Rinks 3 & 4, The Grille offers Philadelphia favorites such as cheesesteaks and soft pretzels, as well as breakfast sandwiches, pizza, burgers, chicken fingers, soups, and salads. Our catering menu provides popular items such as quesadillas, baked ziti, and Asian stir-fry. Contact The Grille by telephone at 610-436-9670 x112 or via email at grille@iceline.info. Goal Line Pub Located within Ice Line Quad Rinks, the Goal Line Pub is a unique restaurant venue that features an extensive food and beverage menu for lunch and dinner in a comfortable, lively environment overlooking the ice. The Goal Line Pub not only caters to the needs of our hardworking teams and their supporters but also is open to all our neighbors in the West Chester community. Whether you’re refueling after a big game, stopping by for a family dinner, or looking for a fantastic place for a lunch break, the Goal Line Pub is the perfect spot for elevated pub cuisine, specialty mixed drinks, and an excellent beer selection. Ice Line strives to maintain consistent times; however special events, tournaments and seasonal youth camps and clinics may disrupt these times. For your convenience, Ice Line provides the following monthly open hockey calendar. Open Hockey sessions are designed to give players seeking extra ice time the opportunity to play in a noncompetitive environment. The maximum amount of players allowed is 30 players plus four goaltenders. Please check the open hockey calendar to confirm dates and times. Full equipment including a certified hockey helmet is required to participate. For more information regarding equipment, please visit the Ice Line Pro Shop or call 610-436-9670 Open Hockey Requirements & Procedures: Player must sign-in before taking the ice. Players who do not sign in may be subject to ejection and banned for future Open Hockey sessions. Sessions limited to the first 30 skaters. All sessions are NON-CHECKING. Recreational play only. Goalies are FREE! (Limited to the first two). Must be 18 years of age or older for all adult sessions. Full equipment and helmet is mandatory (also bring a white and colored jersey). Ice Line reserves the right to revoke a player's privileges due to unsportsmanlike play. Ice Line does not provide pucks. Program Fees Open Hockey Cost: $10 per player

Spectator Info

Ice Line Quad Rinks is a premier, state-of-the-art ice-skating facility in West Chester, PA, offering four NHL-regulation ice surfaces and a wide range of programs for ice skating, figure skating, and ice hockey enthusiasts. Home to club and collegiate programs like the Philadelphia Jr Flyers, Team Philadelphia, West Chester Wolverines and West Chester University, Ice Line regularly hosts adult and youth hockey leagues, camps, clinics, tournaments, and public skating events. With amenities including a full-service pro shop, bar and restaurant, and event space for parties and corporate gatherings, Ice Line serves as a hub for skating entertainment and community engagement.
