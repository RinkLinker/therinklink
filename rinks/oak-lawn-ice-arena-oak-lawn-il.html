About this rink

Operated by Oak Lawn Park District. Year-round. Single sheet with large viewing platform (capacity ~1,100). Recently underwent major makeover (2026). Party rooms, locker rooms, vending machines, private ice rentals. Serves hockey teams, figure skaters, and recreational skaters.
