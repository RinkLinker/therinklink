About this rink

Opened Jan 8, 2000. City-owned. 3,850-seat NHL sheet (home of USHL Cedar Rapids RoughRiders) + Olympic-size public/figure skating sheet. Two sheets on same campus — CSV incorrectly listed as two separate locations. Home of Cedar Rapids Hockey Association, Cedar Rapids High School RoughRiders, Eastern Iowa Figure Skating Club, Cedar Rapids Curling Club, Iowa Speed Skating Association. U of Iowa Hawkeyes club hockey plays here. Formerly ImOn Ice Arena — naming rights expired 2024. New sponsor name not yet announced as of publication.
