About this rink

Part of the 302,000 sq ft Pleasant Prairie RecPlex. Two NHL-sized rinks (85x200) operating year-round. 8 locker rooms. Pro shop on site. Hosts Autumn Ice Classic and CCM Elite Youth tournaments. Also sled hockey (adaptive). Facility includes full waterpark, competition pool, fitness center, and fieldhouse.

Spectator info

Main rink seats 740; second rink seats 350.
