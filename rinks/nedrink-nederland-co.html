About this rink

Olympic-sized outdoor ice rink at Nederland Ice & Racquet Park - claims to be Colorado's largest outdoor ice rink. Non-profit organization staffed by volunteers, established 2005. Open daily Dec-Feb, weather permitting (check webcam on website). Programs: recreational skating, hockey leagues, curling, stick & puck, broomball, group/private lessons ($15/hr). Daily pass: $12 (Ned & Gilpin County residents: $10). Seasonal passes available. Near Frozen Dead Guy Days festival in March. Phone: 720-514-9624.
