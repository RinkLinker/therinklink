About this rink

Single NHL-sized sheet serving Lakewood and Jefferson County. Home of the Foothills Flyers youth hockey association (Littleton, Lakewood and surrounding areas). Strong adult league scene — one of the friendliest drop-in crowds in the Denver metro per community reviews. Website may be intermittent — call (303) 986-4119 to confirm schedule. | On-site shop: Foothills Skate Corner
