About this rink

Official practice facility of Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins (AHL). Located at Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial Park (formerly Coal Street Park). Home of WB Junior Pens, Wilkes University and Wyoming Seminary hockey, Diamond City Figure Skating Club, Anthracite Curling Club. Camps/clinics with Penguins staff. Public skate Fri–Sun. ⚠️ CSV listed in 'Bethlehem PA' — corrected to Wilkes-Barre, PA. Second NHL sheet confirmed per PA rink corrections batch.
