About this rink

Operated by Hoffman Estates Park District (Scott R. Triphahn Community Center). Two NHL sheets. Official practice facility of the Chicago Wolves (AHL). One of the premier Chicagoland facilities. Figure skating and hockey programs ranked among the finest in the area. Public skate, freestyle, skate rental available.
