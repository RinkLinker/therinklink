About this rink

Single sheet, 1000-seat capacity. Previously known as Providence Ice Center, managed by Northern Colorado Youth Hockey since 2007. Home to NCYH Junior Eagles. Also offers curling and adult/youth hockey leagues. Limited public hours during hockey season (Sep-Feb). | On-site shop: NoCo Pro Shop

Spectator info
