About this rink

Two ice surfaces: full-size NHL rink + smaller studio rink. Founded 1992. Only year-round indoor rink in Northern Kentucky. Home of Northern Kentucky Skating Club (NKSC) and Northern Kentucky Youth Hockey Association (NKYHA). Public skating, hockey leagues, figure skating, learn-to-skate. Admission $10, skate rental $3.
