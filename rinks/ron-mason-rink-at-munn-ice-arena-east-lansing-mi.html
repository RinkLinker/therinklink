About this rink

Opened 1974; hockey-only facility. Full name: Clarence L. "Biggie" Munn Ice Arena. Rink named Ron Mason Rink (2022). Capacity 6,114. Home of MSU Spartans (Big Ten; 3 national championships: 1966, 1986, 2007). Held record 323 consecutive regular-season sellouts (1985–2004). 2022 expansion added new locker room, weight room, Ron Mason Hall of Hockey. Pro shop on site. Public skating, open hockey, and freestyle sessions available.
