About this rink

Formerly Leafs Ice Centre. Three NHL sheets. Managed by Canlan Sports (formerly Fairview Facilities Management). Jerry's Pro Shop, BlueLine Bar and Grill restaurant. Off-ice training. Home to Jr. Leafs Youth Hockey (formerly Dundee Dynamites), Leafs Hockey Club, Silver Leafs Figure Skating, Chicago Fusion Synchronized Skating, Chicago Cougars (USPHL), NIU Huskies club hockey, and several HS programs (Crystal Lake South, Mid Valley, Kings). Working relationship with Chicago Blackhawks and Chicago Wolves.
