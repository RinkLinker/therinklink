About this rink

Located in a former Lowry Air Force Base aviation hangar. Two sheets. Home to the Colorado Thunderbirds (Rocky Mountain Junior Hockey League) and multiple high school hockey teams. Public skate sessions: $20/session (1-hour timed sessions), $5 skate rentals. Evening sessions Fri/Sat at 7pm and 9pm. Sessions are booked in advance at bigbearicearena.ezfacility.com - check calendar. Call (303) 343-1111 or email info@bigbearice.com. | On-site shop: Big Bear Skate Shop
