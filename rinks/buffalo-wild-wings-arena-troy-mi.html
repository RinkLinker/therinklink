About this rink

Four NHL-size ice sheets. Formerly Troy Sports Center; now carries Buffalo Wild Wings Arena naming rights. Dance studio and sports rehab facility on site. B&R Sports pro shop on premises. Year-round youth and adult leagues, open skating, drop-in hockey, learn to skate. One of the busiest multi-sheet complexes in Metro Detroit.
