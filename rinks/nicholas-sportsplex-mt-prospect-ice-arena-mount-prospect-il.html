About this rink

104,000 sq ft. Three NHL sheets (North, South, and Reapers rink — third sheet added for Chicago Reapers Tier I club). Home to Northwest Chargers (Tier II), Chicago Reapers (Tier I), and Northern Express youth hockey, plus Saint Viator HS and Notre Dame College Prep. On-site: Jerry's Hockey pro shop, Big Fish's Icehouse / Bricks Wood Fired Pizza Café (second floor restaurant/bar), Health Performance Institute (Illinois Bone & Joint Institute). Parking for 600+ guests.
