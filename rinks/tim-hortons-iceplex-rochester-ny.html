About this rink

176,000 sq ft on Monroe Community College campus. Four NHL-size sheets including Chick-fil-A Arena (1,750 seating). Formerly Bill Gray's Regional Iceplex. AHL Rochester Americans official practice facility. Home of Nazareth Golden Flyers (NCAA D3), Bishop Kearney Girls Hockey, Rochester Coalition, Rochester Edge, Rochester Ice Cats Special Hockey. Chef's Bar & Grill, Lattimore Physical Therapy & Fitness, ScorZone Golf, Visit Rochester Tourism Center on-site. Over 1.5M annual visitors. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Bill Gray's Regional Iceplex' — current sponsor name is Tim Hortons Iceplex. Monroe Community Ice Arena listing in CSV is a duplicate — same facility.
