About this rink

Opened 1999. Formerly Suburban Ice East Lansing; acquired and renamed by Black Bear Sports Group. Two NHL sheets, 14 dressing rooms. 850,000+ visitors annually. Home to Lansing Hockey Club, Biggby AAA Hockey, Lansing Skating Club, Ladies Silver Blades, and five local HS teams. On-site: Perani's Hockey World pro shop, Elevation Hockey training, gym.
