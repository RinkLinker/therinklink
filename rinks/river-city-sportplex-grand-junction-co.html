About this rink

Reopened October 2021 under new ownership after pandemic closure. NHL-sized rink on 4-acre site along the Riverside Trail with Colorado National Monument backdrop. Offers hockey, figure skating, curling, broomball, and sled hockey programs. Home to Grand Junction River Hawks (NCDC junior hockey, joined 2025). Party room packages available for 10-40 guests. | On-site shop: River City Pro Shop
