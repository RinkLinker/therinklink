About this rink

Pagosa Springs' community rink at South Town Park Pavilion. Outdoor seasonal rink with a full program: public skating, drop-in hockey (Saturdays), broomball (Sundays), curling with Riff Raff Brewing (Tuesdays), and figure skating. Skate rentals available. Season passes and punch cards available. Private lessons available — call (970) 398-2337. Email: skatepagosa@icloud.com. Pair with a soak at The Springs Resort natural hot spring complex nearby — a perfect post-skate tradition. Also note: free natural ice skating on Town ponds behind River Center when thick enough (4+ inches), no fee, no rentals.
