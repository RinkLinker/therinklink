About this rink

Opened Sept 2019. $83M public-private facility. 277,000 sq ft. Four NHL-size sheets: feature rink (2,500 seats), Blues practice rink (750 seats), Bob Plager Community Rink (400 seats, sled hockey access), and 'The Barn' covered outdoor rink (4,000-seat grandstand, converts to St. Louis Music Park concert venue in summer). Official practice facility of St. Louis Blues (NHL). Home of Lindenwood University (NCAA D1). Bauer/Pure Hockey retail, 314 Social bar, Mercy sports medicine, 101 ESPN radio studios on-site.
