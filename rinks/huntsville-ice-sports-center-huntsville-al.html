About this rink

Also known as Benton H. Wilcoxon Municipal Ice Complex. Nonprofit facility. Two NHL-regulation sheets. Year-round public skating, figure skating, hockey leagues and tournaments, broomball. Home of Huntsville Skating School, Huntsville Junior Icemen, UAH Club Hockey. Primary hockey facility in Alabama.
