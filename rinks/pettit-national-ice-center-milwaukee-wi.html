About this rink

Official U.S. Olympic Training Facility. One of only 30 indoor 400-meter ovals in the world. Two international-sized rinks plus 400m speed skating oval. Hosts national and world championships. On-site skate rental (figure, hockey, speed skates). Plus Olympic speed skating oval.
