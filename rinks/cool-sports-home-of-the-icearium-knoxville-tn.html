About this rink

Opened Dec 2002 as The Icearium. Expanded 2007 with 40,000 sq ft turf addition. 90,000 sq ft total. One NHL sheet + 2 indoor turf fields. Only facility in East TN with ice and turf under one roof. 30 adult hockey leagues, youth hockey ages 4–18. Only U.S. Figure Skating program in East TN. ⚠️ CSV listed as 'Icearium' — current operating name is Cool Sports Home of the Icearium.
