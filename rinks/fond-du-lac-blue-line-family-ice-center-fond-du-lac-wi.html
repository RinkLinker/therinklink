About this rink

Three sheets: Olympic-sized Rink A (~2,000 seats), NHL-regulation Rink B, and a mini-rink. Non-profit facility. Home to Fondy Bears, Fondy Blades, Fond du Lac Thunder, Marian University, St. Mary's Springs, FDL High School, and Fond du Lac Figure Skating Club. Skate sharpening on site.
