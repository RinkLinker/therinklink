About this rink

Opened January 2017. State-of-the-art full-service complex. Poettker Arena houses 2 NHL sheets with spectator seating; one rink convertible for concerts/events. Also includes 10-lane championship swimming pool, fitness center, walking track, group fitness studios, child care. Home to McKendree University hockey (men's D2, D3, and women's D1 ACHA) and Southern Illinois Ice Hawks youth club. Membership-based with day passes available. Only indoor ice facility in O'Fallon, IL.
