About this rink

Built 1960; one of Madison's two community ice arenas. Ownership transferred to East Madison Ice Collective (nonprofit) in January 2025 following city council vote. Needs ~$3M in repairs by 2028. Home to Patriots Youth Hockey, Madison Eastside Lakers (La Follette/East HS co-op), Monona Grove HS hockey, Figure Skating Club of Madison, and adaptive skating program. Year-round operations. Also home to Wisconsin Whalers (NA3HL).
