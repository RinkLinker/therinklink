About this rink

Dedicated Feb 9, 2020 as Floyd E. Stefanski Ice Center. Only high school-owned two-sheet facility in Ohio. 300,000 visitors/year. Home of Gilmour Lancers varsity hockey + figure skating, 16U and prep teams. Multiple local high schools and a college use as home ice.
