About this rink

35,000 sq ft facility built 1975 (renamed Sertich 1986). NHL regulation ice. 2,000-seat bleachers. Home to Pikes Peak Miners youth hockey. City of Colorado Springs Parks & Recreation. Email: sertichinfo@coloradosprings.gov. Price increases effective Jan 1, 2025.
