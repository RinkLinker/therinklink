About this rink

Seasonal (Sept–March). 5 locker rooms (4 team + 1 female + coaches/referee space). Seating for ~800. Fireplace pavilion and concession stand (Rink Ratz). LiveBarn venue. Home of Rocky River HS hockey. Ice replaced with soccer turf in spring/summer.
