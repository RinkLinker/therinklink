About this rink

Formerly Belknap Ice Arena. Publicly owned facility at Belknap Park; managed by owners of the Grand Rapids Griffins (Detroit Red Wings AHL affiliate). Official practice facility of the Grand Rapids Griffins. Home to Grand Rapids Amateur Hockey Association (GRAHA), Griffins Youth Foundation hockey, and Keyes Institute of Ice Hockey. Lunch skate available year-round, plus open skating, adult leagues, and drop-in hockey.
