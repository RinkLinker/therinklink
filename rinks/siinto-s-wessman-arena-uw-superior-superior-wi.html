About this rink

Built 1969; regarded as one of the top NCAA DIII hockey facilities in the nation. Ice open October through mid-March; dry floor events rest of year. Home to UW-Superior Yellowjackets men's and women's hockey (NCAA DIII) and Superior High School boys hockey. Has hosted 4 NCAA DIII championships (1994, 2000, 2007, 2013). Recent renovations include new boards/glass, icemaking equipment, flooring, and sound system.
