About this rink

9,500-seat ECHL arena opened 2022. Home of Savannah Ghost Pirates (ECHL, Florida Panthers affiliate). Public skating program launched Nov 2025 per City of Savannah agreement with OVG and Ghost Pirates. Youth hockey and adult league ice also available. $20 adults / $15 kids 12 & under. Replaced Martin Luther King Jr. Arena as primary Savannah hockey venue.
