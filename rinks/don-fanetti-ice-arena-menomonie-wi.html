About this rink

Located at Dunn County Recreation Park. Also known as Dunn County Ice Arena. Owned and managed by the Menomonie Ice Board (non-profit). Community rink serving Menomonie youth and high school hockey programs since the 1970s. Expanded 2014 to include the Fanetti Community Center addition.
